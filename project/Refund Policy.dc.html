Refund Policy

Last updated June 2026 · Sample policy text for design purposes.

Terms of Use Privacy Policy Refund Policy Disclaimers

We want you to learn something useful. This policy explains when paid courses, certificates, and group seats are eligible for a refund.

Individual courses & certificates

Request a refund within 14 days of purchase if you've completed less than 25% of the course. Once a certificate has been issued, that purchase is non-refundable.

Free courses

Free courses have no charge and therefore no refund.

Group seats

Unused seats may be refundable within 14 days of purchase. Seats that have been assigned to a learner who has started a course are non-refundable for that term.

Custom training

Custom training is scoped and quoted individually; refund terms are set out in each engagement agreement.

How to request

Email help@byraeform.com with your account email and the purchase in question. We'll respond within a few business days.

Need help with a purchase? Visit the Help Center.
