What a group account is

A group account is a paid organization workspace. You buy seats, invite your people, and assign the exact courses and paths they need. Everyone learns the same system, and you see progress in real time.

It's built for leaders who are responsible for getting a team trained — not just for individual learners.

Group account FAQ

Can I add more seats later?▾

Yes. You can buy additional seats at any time from your admin dashboard, and assign them to new learners immediately.

Can I reassign a seat if someone leaves?▾

Yes. Mark a learner inactive to free their seat, then invite a replacement. Completed certificates stay with the original learner.

Do learners get their own dashboard?▾

Yes. Each learner sees their assigned courses and progress in a personal dashboard, while you see the whole team in the admin view.

Can we get training built for our organization?▾

Yes — that's RaeLearn Custom. We build a tailored learning plan and private training around your real systems. Request custom training →
