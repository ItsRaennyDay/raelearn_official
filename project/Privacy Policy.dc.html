This policy explains what information RaeLearn collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. We aim to collect only what we need to run the service well.

What we collect

Account details (name, email), learning activity (courses, progress, completions), and, for group accounts, the organization profile and learner roster the admin provides.

How we use it

To deliver courses, track progress, issue certificates, provide group reporting to admins, and improve the platform. We do not sell your personal information.

Group accounts

If you join through a group account, your admin can see your assigned courses, progress, completions, and certificates. Personal account activity outside the group remains private.

Your choices

You can update your profile, request a copy of your data, or ask us to delete your account by contacting us. Some records may be retained where required.

Privacy questions? Email help@byraeform.com or visit the Help Center.
