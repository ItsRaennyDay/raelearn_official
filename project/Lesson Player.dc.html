In this lesson

Segmentation is how a clean donor list turns into useful data. You'll learn the three tag families every nonprofit CRM needs — giving level, donor type, and engagement — and how to apply them consistently so reports and acknowledgements stay accurate. We'll finish by spot-checking your work to catch bulk-tagging mistakes early.

Side note

Tags live on the donor record, not the gift — so they travel with the donor across every donation.

Tip from the field

Decide your tag list before you start tagging. Inventing tags as you go is the #1 cause of messy segments.

Key takeaways
1

Define a fixed tag list before tagging anyone.

2

Use three families: giving level, donor type, engagement.

3

Apply tags in bulk, then spot-check 10 records.
