Accounts & access

How do I set up an account?▾

Create a free individual account from any Start Learning button. Leaders training a team should create a group account instead.

How do I access a course I enrolled in?▾

Everything you've enrolled in appears on your learner dashboard under Continue learning, with progress saved automatically.

How does payment work?▾

Free courses need no card. Paid courses and certificates are purchased individually; group accounts are billed by seats.

Courses & certificates

How do I earn a certificate?▾

Complete every course in the certificate track and pass the final assessment. Your certificate then becomes available to download.

Where do I download my certificate?▾

From the Certificates panel on your learner dashboard. Group admins can also see earned certificates per learner.

Group accounts

How do I invite learners?▾

From your admin dashboard, use Invite learners and add emails. Each invited learner takes one seat until you mark them inactive.

How do I assign courses and paths?▾

Select learners and assign individual courses or a full learning path. Assignments appear on each learner's dashboard right away.

How do I get progress reports?▾

Export a team progress report, completion summary, or certificate register as CSV or PDF from the Reports panel.
